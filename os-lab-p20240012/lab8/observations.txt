Explanation:
The final inventory is significantly greater than 0 because of a Time-of-Check to Time-of-Use (TOC-TOU) race condition. Multiple concurrent buy_widget processes read the same inventory value (e.g. 100) before any of them write the updated value (e.g. 98). As a result, many updates are lost (overwritten by other processes), and some executions even read an empty file while another process is in the middle of truncating/writing to it, causing integer comparison errors.
