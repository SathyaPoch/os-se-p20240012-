--- Zombie and Orphan Cleanup Notes ---
Q: How are orphans cleaned up? A: They are adopted by init (PID 1), which reaps them when they finish.
Q: How are zombies cleaned up? A: The parent calls wait(), or if the parent is killed, init adopts and reaps them.
Q: Can you kill a zombie with 'kill -9'? Why or why not? A: No, because zombies are already dead and only exist as entries in the process table.
